During this course five video presentations are to be handed in. (Two individual presentations, three group presentations.)

Put the files for these videos in the `presentations/` directory, giving them the right name. Then, commit the file and push to GitLab.

- idea.mp4: Early game idea. Start of week 2, individual. Max 60s.
- prototype.mp4: Prototype pitch. Start of week 3, individual. Max 90s.
- sprint1.mp4: Sprint 1 demo. Start of week 6, group. Max 6m.
- sprint2.mp4: Sprint 2 demo. Start of week 8, group. Max 6m.
- appshow.mp4: App Show trailer. The day before the App Show, group. Max 60s.
